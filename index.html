Practical

Lost Galaxy is a science fiction fantasy tabletop roleplaying game where players are given the freedom to explore the endless adventures of the galaxy. Mixing traditional fantasy and classic science fiction into a unique game experience that neither could offer on its own, Lost Galaxy van offers a fun and exciting experience for fans of both.
